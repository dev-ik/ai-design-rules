# A11Y-001 44x44 Touch Targets

Status: Draft
Domain: Accessibility

## Source Research

- `research/products/apple-reminders.md`
- `research/products/things-3.md`

## Why

Fast mobile actions fail when controls are too small to hit reliably. A product can look clean and still be difficult to use if repeated actions require precise tapping.

## Rule

Interactive touch targets must be at least 44x44 CSS pixels unless the platform has a stricter local standard.

## Good

- Primary and repeated controls meet the minimum target size.
- Small icons sit inside a larger clickable area.
- Adjacent controls have enough spacing to avoid accidental taps.
- Disabled controls remain visually clear.

## Bad

- Icon-only buttons use the icon size as the hit area.
- Dense lists place destructive and positive actions too close together.
- Mobile controls shrink below readable and tappable size to fit more content.

## Review

- Inspect every interactive control on the mobile layout.
- Measure the clickable area, not only the visible icon.
- Check dense list rows, toolbars, and bottom navigation.
- Confirm keyboard and screen reader labels still exist where relevant.
