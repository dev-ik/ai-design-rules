# IA-001 One Stable Home Surface

Status: Draft
Domain: IA

## Source Research

- `research/products/telegram.md`
- `research/products/things-3.md`
- `research/products/apple-reminders.md`

## Why

Products with many modes become understandable when users can return to one stable home surface. The home surface anchors memory and reduces the cost of switching tasks.

## Rule

Give the product one primary home surface that stays recognizable as features, modes, and organization layers grow.

## Good

- Users can name where they return after finishing a task.
- New modes extend the home model instead of replacing it.
- Navigation supports fast exit and fast return.
- Advanced grouping is optional.

## Bad

- Each feature introduces a new top-level destination with a different mental model.
- The product has several competing "home" screens.
- Users must remember which mode they were in to find basic work.

## Review

- Identify the product's home surface in one sentence.
- Check whether each major mode returns to that surface.
- Check whether new organization adds clarity or creates another product inside the product.
