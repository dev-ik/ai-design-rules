# UX-001 Thumb-Zone Primary Actions

Status: Draft
Domain: UX

## Source Research

- `research/products/things-3.md`
- `research/products/apple-reminders.md`

## Why

Mobile consumer workflows depend on fast repeated actions. If the main action sits outside comfortable reach, the product feels slower even when the feature set is correct.

## Rule

Place the primary mobile action where it is reachable by thumb and keep destructive or secondary actions away from accidental taps.

## Good

- The main action is reachable near the bottom or another established thumb-friendly control area.
- Secondary actions are visually and spatially distinct.
- Repeated list actions support quick insertion, completion, and reordering.

## Bad

- The primary action is only in a top bar on a tall mobile viewport.
- Multiple actions compete in the same touch area.
- Destructive actions sit next to frequent positive actions.

## Review

- Test the screen at 390px width.
- Confirm the primary action is reachable without hand repositioning.
- Confirm touch targets meet `A11Y-001`.
- Confirm the layout still works when the list is empty and when it is long.
