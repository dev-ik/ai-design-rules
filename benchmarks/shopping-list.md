# Shopping List Benchmark

## Product Brief

Create a modern consumer shopping list app for shared household use.

The app should help users add items quickly, mark items bought, group or scan items, and recover from sync or save failures.

## Expected User

- Consumer shopping for household items.
- Often uses the app one-handed on mobile.

## Expected Platform

- Responsive web app.
- Mobile-first behavior required.

## Constraints

- No ecommerce checkout.
- No delivery tracking.
- No heavy inventory system.
- Include empty, loading, error, and completed item states.
- Item capture should not require category or assignee first.

## Evaluation Criteria

- Product Thinking
- UX
- Information Architecture
- Accessibility
- Mobile-first
- State Design
- Simplicity
- Performance Awareness
- Overall Product Quality
