# Figma Treats Motion As A Design System Capability

## Source

Figma Motion launch materials from Config 2026.

## Observed Behavior

Figma places timelines, easing, timing, and keyframes beside components and variables. Motion can be reused through systems, inspected during handoff, and exported to implementation formats instead of being described informally after static screens are complete.

## Why It May Matter

Motion becomes more reliable when its purpose and parameters are part of the component or transition contract. Agents should specify the state relationship, implementation values, and reduced-motion behavior rather than request generic polish.
