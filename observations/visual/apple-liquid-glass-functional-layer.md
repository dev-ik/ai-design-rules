# Apple Uses Liquid Glass As A Functional Layer

## Source

Apple Human Interface Guidelines for materials and the WWDC26 design guide.

## Observed Behavior

Apple describes Liquid Glass as a distinct layer for controls and navigation above content. The guidance warns against using the material throughout the content layer and recommends applying custom glass effects sparingly, with accessibility settings such as reduced transparency and increased contrast preserved.

## Why It May Matter

The visible trend is not a license to cover every surface with transparency. Its reusable signal is that expressive material can separate interaction from content when hierarchy, legibility, and platform behavior remain clear.
